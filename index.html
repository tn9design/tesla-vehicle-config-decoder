Send Us Your Tesla Config URL

If your exact model-year options are missing here, the fastest way to help expand support is to send the Tesla image/config URL for your car.

  1. Log in to your Tesla account at tesla.com/teslaaccount.
  2. Open your vehicle details or any place Tesla shows the image/config thumbnail for that car.
  3. Right-click the vehicle image and choose the browser option that copies the image link or copies the image address/path.
  4. Paste that full Tesla URL into a GitHub issue so we can decode the missing options and add support later.

Helpful details to include: model, year, trim, wheel type, interior color, and anything else that makes your configuration unusual.
